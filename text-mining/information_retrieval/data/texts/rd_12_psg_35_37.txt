<sent id="35">  Giovanni Battista Somis was born in Piedmont , and , after studying under Corelli , he went to Venice and studied under Vivaldi . </sent> <sent id="36">  He was appointed solo violinist to the king at Turin and leader of the royal band , and seems scarcely ever to have left Turin after these appointments . </sent> <sent id="37">  Little is known of his playing or his compositions , but , by the work of his pupils , it is evident that he possessed originality . </sent>
