<sent id="63">  Francesco Geminiani was considered the ablest of the pupils of Corelli , and was born about 1680 . </sent> <sent id="64">  When about twenty four years of age he went to England , where his talent secured a great reputation for him , some people even declaring him to be superior , as a player , to Corelli . </sent> <sent id="65">  He lived to an advanced age , and was in Dublin visiting his pupil Dubourg at the time of his death . </sent>
