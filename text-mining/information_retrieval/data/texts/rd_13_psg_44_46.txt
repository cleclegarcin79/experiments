<sent id="44">  Shrinkage is particularly early and prominent in the hippocampi , structures that are needed for making new memories , and in the posterior parietal lobes , which are involved in spatial skills , navigation , and calculation . </sent> <sent id="45">  Later , nearly the entire brain appears shrunken , and the spaces within the brain , called ventricles , grow larger . </sent> <sent id="46">  Memory and spatial problems are usually the first symptoms seen and are disproportionate to problems with attention , organizing , and multi tasking . </sent>
