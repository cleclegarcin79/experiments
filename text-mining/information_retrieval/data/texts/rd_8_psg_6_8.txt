<sent id="6">  About jatropha : Jatropha is a small tree that grows to 3 5 metres in height and a member of the Euphorbiaceae family . </sent> <sent id="7">  It is native to Central America but is now grown in many parts of the tropics and subtropics . </sent> <sent id="8">  The seeds , which contain up to 35 per cent oil , can be processed into biodiesel for transport and biofuel for lighting and cooking . </sent>
