<sent id="53">  Soon after this a special band called Les Petits Violons was formed with Lulli at their head , and under his direction it surpassed the band of twenty four . </sent> <sent id="54">  Lulli found great favor at court , and , indeed , astonished the world with his exquisite taste and skill . </sent> <sent id="55">  That he was firmly established in the favor of the king is shown by the story that , when Corelli came to France and played one of his sonatas , King Louis listened without showing any sign of pleasure , and , sending for one of his own violinists , requested him to play an aria from Lulli 's opera of `` Cadmus et Hermione , '' which , he declared , suited his taste . </sent>
