<sent id="43">  In Brazil , emissions from raising cattle are as important as energy sector emissions . </sent> <sent id="44">  Farm animals release methane , and according to the Ministry of Science and Technology , in 1995 these emissions were in the order of ten megatonnes , 80 per cent of which came from beef cattle . </sent> <sent id="45">  Converted to carbon dioxide equivalent , that is equal to 233.7 megatonnes - only slightly lower than the 248.4 megatonnes of greenhouse gases emitted by the energy sector the previous year . </sent>
