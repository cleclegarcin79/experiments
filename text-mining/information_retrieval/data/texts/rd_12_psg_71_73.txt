<sent id="71">  Antonio Vivaldi was the son of a violinist , and sought his fortune in Germany , but returned to his native city in 1713 . </sent> <sent id="72">  He wrote extensively for the violin , and is said to have added something to the development of its technique . </sent> <sent id="73">  An anecdote is told of him to the effect that one day during mass a theme for a fugue struck him . </sent>
