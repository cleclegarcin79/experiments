<sent id="18">  A more common genetic risk is associated with the apolipoprotein gene on chromosome 19 . </sent> <sent id="19">  This gene has three common variants or alleles , called APOE e2 , APOE e3 , and APOE e4 . </sent> <sent id="20">  Individuals with 1 or 2 copies of the APOE e4 allele have a substantially increased risk for AD , whereas individuals with 1 or 2 copies of the APOE e2 allele have decreased risk . </sent>
