<sent id="16">  Transpiring plants , especially forests , work as a kind of biotic pump , causing humid air to be sucked out of the ocean and transferred to dry land . </sent> <sent id="17">  If the vegetation is removed from the land , this natural system of biosphere regulation is interrupted . </sent> <sent id="18">  Soil erodes , reducing the content of organic material in the ground , thus reducing its ability to hold water . </sent>
