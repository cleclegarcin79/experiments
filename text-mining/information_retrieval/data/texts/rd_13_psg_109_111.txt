<sent id="109">  Treatment : Once a diagnosis of AD has been made , treatment of the disease should be considered . </sent> <sent id="110">  Treatment has many components and should focus around the following : Symptomatic therapy with medicationso Treatment of difficult behaviors with medications ; Treatment of difficult behaviors with changes in the environment ; Organizing finances , legal issues , advanced directives ; Making sure that the caregiver maintains his or her health Symptomatic therapy with medications : Currently there are two types of medications approved for the treatment of AD , cholinesterase inhibitors and NMDA antagonists . </sent> <sent id="111">  Cholinesterase inhibitors work by increasing the brain 's level of acetylcholine , and they are the mainstay of AD treatment . </sent>
