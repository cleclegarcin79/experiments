<sent id="1">  Famous Violinist of Today and Yesterday , Arcangelo Corelli , whose name is recognized as one of the greatest in the history of violin playing and composition , and who laid the foundation for all future development of technique , was born in 1653 , at Fusignano , near Imola , in the territory of Bologna . </sent> <sent id="2">  He showed an early propensity for the violin , and studied under Bassani , a man of extensive knowledge and capabilities , while Mattei Simonelli was his instructor in counterpoint . </sent> <sent id="3">  Corelli at one time sought fame away from home , and he is said to have visited Paris , where Lulli , the chief violinist of that city , exhibited such jealousy and violence that the mild tempered Corelli withdrew . </sent>
